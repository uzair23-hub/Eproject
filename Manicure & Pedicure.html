Basic Manicure Services

A Basic Manicure is a simple yet essential nail care treatment that keeps your hands and nails looking clean, healthy, and polished. It involves several key steps to enhance the overall appearance of your nails while providing relaxation and nourishment to your hands.
Step-by-Step Process:
Hand Soaking – The process starts with soaking your hands in warm, soapy water to soften the skin and cuticles.
Nail Trimming & Shaping – Nails are trimmed to the desired length and shaped into a square, round, almond, or coffin shape.
Cuticle Care – Cuticles are gently pushed back and trimmed (if needed) to create a neat and clean look.
Exfoliation (Optional) – Some basic manicures include a gentle scrub to remove dead skin and promote smooth hands.
Moisturizing Massage – Hands are massaged with lotion or oil to hydrate and relax the skin.
Base Coat Application – A protective base coat is applied to prevent staining and strengthen the nails.
Nail Polish (Optional) – A regular nail polish of your choice is applied in layers for a glossy finish.
Top Coat & Drying – A top coat is applied to seal the color and add shine, followed by a quick-dry process.

Benefits of a Basic Manicure:
Keeps nails clean, strong, and well-groomed.
Prevents nail damage, breakage, and uneven growth.
Promotes blood circulation through a relaxing hand massage.
Hydrates hands and cuticles, preventing dryness and cracking.
Enhances the overall appearance of hands, making them look elegant and polished.

Gel Manicure Services

A French Manicure is a timeless and elegant nail style characterized by a natural-looking base with crisp white tips. It gives the nails a clean, polished, and sophisticated appearance, making it a popular choice for weddings, work settings, and everyday elegance. This classic manicure complements any outfit and occasion while providing a well-groomed and stylish look.


Step-by-Step Process:
Nail Preparation – The nails are cleaned, trimmed, and filed into the desired shape (square, round, oval, almond, or coffin)
Cuticle Care – Cuticles are gently pushed back and any excess skin is removed for a neat and tidy appearance.
Base Coat Application – A clear or sheer pink/nude base coat is applied to enhance the natural nail color and protect the nail plate.
White Tip Painting – The tips of the nails are carefully painted white, either freehand or using guides/tape for precision.
Second Coat for Perfection – A second layer of white polish may be added to intensify the color and ensure an even finish. Base Coat Application – A protective base coat is applied to prevent staining and strengthen the nails.
Blending & Sealing – A sheer pink or nude polish may be applied over the entire nail to create a seamless blend between the white tip and the base.
Top Coat Application – A glossy or matte top coat is applied to protect the manicure and add shine or a modern matte finish.

Variations of French Manicure:
Classic French Manicure – Sheer pink or nude base with crisp white tips.
Reverse French Manicure – A color or white accent along the cuticle instead of the tip.
Glitter French Manicure – White tips replaced with glitter or shimmer for a glamorous look
Colored French Tips – Instead of white, the tips are painted in trendy colors like black, red, or neon shades.
Matte French Manicure – Uses a matte top coat instead of a glossy finish for a modern touch. Benefits of a French Manicure: ✔ Timeless and versatile look suitable for any occasion. ✔ Enhances the natural beauty of the nails. ✔ Works well on both short and long nails. ✔ Can be customized with different styles and colors. ✔ Gives nails a neat and polished appearance with minimal effort.

Relaxation: The warm soak and massage help to relieve stress and tension in your feet and legs, promoting overall relaxation.
Soft and Smooth Skin: Exfoliation removes dead skin cells and rough patches, giving you smooth and soft feet.
Nail Health: Regular nail trimming and cuticle care help prevent discomfort and infections, and ensure that your toenails stay in good condition.
Aesthetic Appeal: Polishing adds a beautiful touch, and it’s a great way to keep your feet looking elegant and well-groomed. Enhanced Circulation: The massage included in the pedicure helps stimulate blood circulation in the feet and legs, which can be beneficial, especially for individuals who spend long hours on their feet. French cut Pedicure Services

An Acrylic Pedicure is an advanced and durable pedicure treatment that focuses on enhancing the appearance of your toenails with acrylic nails. This pedicure is ideal for individuals looking to correct nail imperfections, add length, or achieve a flawless, long-lasting finish. Acrylic nails are applied to the natural toenails using a combination of liquid monomer and powder polymer, creating a strong and flexible coating that can last for weeks.


Step-by-Step Process:
Soaking & Cleaning:
Your feet are first soaked in warm, soothing water to soften the skin and nails. Any dirt or debris is gently removed, and the nails are cleaned thoroughly.
Exfoliation:
Exfoliation is performed on the feet to remove dead skin and calluses, leaving the feet feeling soft and smooth.
Cuticle Care:
The cuticles are pushed back and trimmed to ensure clean nail beds, allowing for better adhesion of the acrylic nails.
Acrylic Nail Application:
A thin layer of acrylic is applied to each toenail. The acrylic is mixed using a liquid monomer and powder polymer, then molded over the nail to create an even, smooth surface. The acrylic is shaped to your desired length and style.
Shaping & Filing:
After the acrylic has dried, your nails are carefully shaped and filed to the desired length and shape. Common shapes include square, oval, or almond.

Benefits of Acrylic Pedicure Services:
Long-Lasting Durability: Acrylic nails are highly durable and can last for several weeks without chipping or breaking, making them ideal for those with an active lifestyle or busy schedule.
Nail Protection: The acrylic layer protects your natural nails from damage, such as splitting, cracking, or breaking. It’s especially helpful for individuals with weak or brittle toenails.
Customization & Design: Acrylic pedicures offer endless possibilities for customization, whether you want a simple color, French tips, or more complex designs like ombre, glitter, or 3D art. You can create unique and stylish looks that suit your personality.
Nail Length & Shape: If your toenails are short or damaged, acrylics can add length and help you achieve the desired nail shape and appearance.
Resistant to Wear & Tear: Unlike regular nail polish, acrylic nails are highly resistant to chips, smudges, and wear and tear. They remain looking perfect for weeks.

Gel Pedicure Services

A Gel Pedicure is a premium pedicure service that uses gel polish to provide a long-lasting, glossy, and durable finish. Unlike regular nail polish, gel polish is cured under a UV or LED light, which makes it stronger, more flexible, and longer-lasting. This pedicure is ideal for those looking for vibrant, chip-resistant nails that last for weeks without fading or smudging.


Step-by-Step Process:
Soaking & Cleaning:
The feet are soaked in warm, relaxing water to soften the skin and nails. This step helps remove dirt and prepares the nails for the pedicure.
Exfoliation:
A gentle scrub is applied to exfoliate the feet, removing dead skin cells and rough patches, especially around the heels and toes.
Cuticle Care:
The cuticles are softened, pushed back, and trimmed to provide a clean base for applying the gel polish. This ensures a smooth, neat appearance around the nail.
Nail Shaping:
The toenails are trimmed and shaped to your desired style. Common shapes include square, oval, or round.
Gel Base Coat:
A thin layer of gel base coat is applied to each toenail. This layer helps the gel polish adhere better to the natural nail.

Benefits of Gel Pedicure Services:
Long-Lasting Durability: Gel polish is highly durable and resistant to chips, cracks, and smudges. It can last for 2-3 weeks without losing its shine or vibrancy, making it perfect for those with busy schedules or vacations.
Instant Drying: Unlike regular nail polish, gel polish is cured under a UV or LED light, which means there’s no drying time. You can leave the salon with your pedicure completely dry and smudge-free.
Chip-Resistant: Gel polish is much more resistant to everyday wear and tear, such as scratching or chipping, which makes it ideal for individuals who want their pedicure to stay perfect for longer.
Glossy Finish: Gel nails have a high-gloss, shiny finish that remains intact throughout the life of the pedicure, giving your feet a polished and elegant appearance.
Perfect for Special Occasions: Whether you're preparing for a wedding, vacation, or any special event, a gel pedicure ensures that your nails stay looking fresh and beautiful without the need for constant touch-ups.

Luxury Pedicure Services

A Luxury Pedicure is the ultimate indulgence for your feet, combining relaxation, rejuvenation, and pampering with high-end treatments and premium products. This type of pedicure is designed to provide a spa-like experience, focusing not only on the appearance of your feet and nails but also on overall relaxation and skin health. It typically includes additional steps, luxurious products, and longer treatment time compared to regular pedicures.


Step-by-Step Process:
Soaking & Foot Scrub:
The pedicure begins with a relaxing foot soak in warm water infused with essential oils, bath salts, or fragrant lotions. The soak softens the skin, relieves tension, and prepares your feet for the rest of the treatment. This step is followed by a luxurious exfoliating scrub that helps to remove dead skin, smooth calluses, and reveal softer feet.
Foot Mask:
After exfoliation, a rich, hydrating foot mask is applied. These masks are often made from ingredients such as honey, coconut, shea butter, or collagen. The mask deeply moisturizes, nourishes, and rejuvenates the skin, providing a glowing and healthy appearance.
Cuticle Care:
The cuticles are carefully pushed back and trimmed to ensure a clean, neat look around the toenails. This process promotes healthy nail growth and ensures that the nails are properly prepped for polish application.
Nail Shaping & Buffing:
The toenails are trimmed and shaped according to your preferences, whether you prefer square, oval, or almond-shaped nails. They are then buffed to a smooth, even finish, eliminating any rough edges or imperfections.
Luxury Massage:
One of the key features of a luxury pedicure is a long, relaxing foot and lower leg massage. Using rich lotions or oils, the massage not only relaxes tired muscles but also helps to improve circulation, reduce swelling, and relieve foot pain. This massage is often combined with hot stones or a warm towel for extra relaxation.

Benefits of Luxury Pedicure Services:
Deep Hydration & Nourishment: The use of high-quality products like rich foot masks, oils, and creams deeply nourishes the skin, providing long-lasting hydration. This is especially beneficial for dry, cracked, or rough feet.
Enhanced Relaxation: The extended foot and leg massage with essential oils, hot stones, or towels provides ultimate relaxation, relieving stress and tension. It also improves blood circulation, reducing swelling and fatigue in the feet and legs.
Improved Skin Texture: Regular exfoliation, moisturizing, and nourishing foot treatments result in smooth, soft, and healthy skin. The luxurious products used in the process rejuvenate and maintain the skin’s elasticity.
Increased Nail Health: The detailed cuticle care, nail shaping, and buffing promote healthy toenail growth and ensure your nails are strong and well-maintained. The luxury pedicure helps to prevent ingrown nails, hangnails, and other common nail problems.
Long-Lasting Results: Luxury pedicures often include high-end polishes or gel application, providing long-lasting, chip-resistant nails that maintain their shine and color for weeks. This makes them perfect for special occasions, holidays, or for those who want beautiful feet for an extended period.
